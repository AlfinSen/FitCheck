# User Guide

## Navigating the App

### Home Page
The landing page showcases the latest trends and features.
-   **Hero Carousel**: Automatically cycles through featured styles. You can pause the carousel or manually switch slides using the controls at the bottom.
-   **Navigation**: Use the top bar to navigate. On mobile, tap the hamburger menu icon (top left) to open the full menu.
-   **Try It Now**: Click the "Try it now" button to enter the Virtual Try-On Studio.

## Using the Virtual Try-On Studio

1.  **Upload Your Photo**:
    -   Click the "Upload Photo" area or drag and drop an image file.
    -   **Tip**: For best results, use a clear, well-lit full-body photo where you are facing the camera.

2.  **Select an Outfit**:
    -   Browse the "Choose your style" grid on the right.
    -   Click on an item (e.g., Red T-Shirt, Blue Hoodie) to select it.
    -   The selected item will be highlighted with a blue border.

3.  **Generate**:
    -   Once both a photo and an outfit are selected, the "Generate" button in the top right header will become active.
    -   Click "Generate".
    -   Wait a few seconds for the AI to analyze your photo and apply the outfit.

4.  **View Results**:
    -   The processed image will appear in the main preview area.
    -   To try a different outfit, simply select a new item and click "Generate" again.
    -   To use a different photo, click the "X" icon in the top right of the preview to clear the current image.
